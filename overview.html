概要

天然物有機化学分野では，有機化学を通して，天然物の可能性を探ることを目的に，天然物の有機合成と生理活性評価を中心に研究活動を行っています．

Outline

The purpose of our research is to develop new functional molecules that can be used for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, agricultural chemicals, etc. through structural analysis of natural products created by living organisms, organic synthesis, derivative synthesis, and biological evaluation.
